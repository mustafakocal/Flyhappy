Spider-Man Variants

Peter Parker (Original Spider-Man)

Peter Parker is the original Spider-Man, a teenager who was bitten by a radioactive spider, gaining superhuman abilities. He uses these powers to fight crime in New York City.

Spider-Man 2099 (Miguel O'Hara)

Miguel O'Hara is a geneticist in the year 2099 who accidentally becomes Spider-Man while trying to recreate the abilities of the original Spider-Man, Peter Parker.

Spider-Gwen (Gwen Stacy)

In an alternate universe, Gwen Stacy is bitten by a radioactive spider and becomes Spider-Woman, also known as Spider-Gwen. She fights crime while balancing her life as a teenager.

Spider-Man Noir

Spider-Man Noir is a dark and gritty version of Spider-Man from an alternate universe set in the 1930s. He operates in a noir-inspired world filled with crime and corruption.

Miles Morales (Ultimate Spider-Man)

Miles Morales is a teenager who takes up the mantle of Spider-Man after the death of Peter Parker in the Ultimate Marvel universe. He possesses similar powers to Peter, along with unique abilities like camouflage and venom blasts.

Ben Reilly (Scarlet Spider)

Ben Reilly is a clone of Peter Parker who takes on the identity of the Scarlet Spider. He has similar powers to Peter and often struggles with his sense of identity.

Spider-Woman (Jessica Drew)

Jessica Drew was exposed to a spider-based serum while still in the womb, giving her powers similar to Spider-Man, along with bio-electric energy blasts and immunity to poisons.

Spider-Punk (Hobie Brown)

Hobie Brown is a rebellious Spider-Man from an alternate universe who uses his punk rock attitude and his abilities to fight against oppression in a dystopian society.

Superior Spider-Man (Doctor Octopus)

After swapping minds with Peter Parker, Doctor Octopus attempts to prove he can be a better Spider-Man, taking on the role of the Superior Spider-Man with a more ruthless approach to crime-fighting.

Spider-Man 1602

In this reimagining, Peter Parquagh is a young apprentice in the year 1602 who gains spider-like abilities in a world filled with Elizabethan-era versions of Marvel characters.

Spider-Ham (Peter Porker)

Spider-Ham, also known as Peter Porker, is an anthropomorphic pig version of Spider-Man from a cartoon-like alternate universe where animals are the dominant species.

Spider-Man India (Pavitr Prabhakar)

Pavitr Prabhakar is a young boy from Mumbai who gains spider-like abilities and becomes Spider-Man in his world, battling local crime and supervillains.

Spider-Ma'am (Aunt May)

In an alternate universe, Aunt May is the one bitten by the radioactive spider, gaining the powers of Spider-Man and becoming Spider-Ma'am.

Cosmic Spider-Man

When Peter Parker temporarily gains the powers of the Uni-Power, he becomes the nearly omnipotent Cosmic Spider-Man, capable of feats far beyond those of a regular Spider-Man.

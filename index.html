Spider-Man Variants

Peter Parker (Original Spider-Man)

Peter Parker is the original Spider-Man, a teenager who was bitten by a radioactive spider, gaining superhuman abilities. He uses these powers to fight crime in New York City.

Spider-Man 2099 (Miguel O'Hara)

Miguel O'Hara is a geneticist in the year 2099 who accidentally becomes Spider-Man while trying to recreate the abilities of the original Spider-Man, Peter Parker.

Spider-Gwen (Gwen Stacy)

In an alternate universe, Gwen Stacy is bitten by a radioactive spider and becomes Spider-Woman, also known as Spider-Gwen. She fights crime while balancing her life as a teenager.

Spider-Man Noir

Spider-Man Noir is a dark and gritty version of Spider-Man from an alternate universe set in the 1930s. He operates in a noir-inspired world filled with crime and corruption.

Miles Morales (Ultimate Spider-Man)

Miles Morales is a teenager who takes up the mantle of Spider-Man after the death of Peter Parker in the Ultimate Marvel universe. He possesses similar powers to Peter, along with unique abilities like camouflage and venom blasts.
